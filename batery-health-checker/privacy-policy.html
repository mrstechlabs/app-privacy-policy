Privacy Policy

App Name: Battery Health Checker

Last updated: 30 December 2025

Battery Health Checker respects your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how information is handled when you use our mobile application.

Information Collection and Use

Battery Health Checker does not collect, store, or share any personally identifiable information from users.

The app functions offline and only accesses system-provided battery information available on your device to display battery-related data.

Third-Party Services

The app may use third-party services that may collect information used to identify you. These services are used to improve app functionality and support monetization.

  • Google AdMob (for displaying advertisements)
  • Google Play Services
  • Expo platform services

Please review the privacy policies of these third-party providers to understand how they handle user data.

Log Data

In case of an error or crash, the app may collect log data through third-party tools. This data may include device information, operating system version, app version, and the time and date of usage.

This information is used solely for improving app performance and stability.

Security

We value your trust and strive to use commercially acceptable means of protecting your information. However, no method of transmission over the internet or electronic storage is 100% secure.

Children’s Privacy

Battery Health Checker does not knowingly collect any personal information from children under the age of 13. If you believe that your child has provided personal information, please contact us so we can take appropriate action.

Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page. Users are advised to review this page periodically.
